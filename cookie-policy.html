Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website to remember your preferences and provide personalized features.

What Cookies Do We Use?

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to track and analyze website traffic. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, which pages are visited most frequently, and to identify areas for improvement.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as your preferred language or region, to provide you with a more personalized experience.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please keep in mind that removing or disabling cookies may impact your user experience on our website.

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to disable or block certain types of cookies, or to be notified when a website tries to set or update a cookie.
  • Third-Party Tools: You can also use third-party tools to manage cookies, such as browser extensions or privacy-focused plugins.
